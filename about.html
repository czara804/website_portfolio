I grew up in country Victoria, in the Mallee, near the Murray river. As a child of farmers I spent my childhood running through grapevines, riding motorbikes, reading and dancing.

I went to school in Mildura, Victoria and moved to Melbourne to complete a Bachelor of Science at the University of Melbourne majoring in Microbiology, Infectious Disease & Immunology. I completed a Master of Teaching with majors in VCE Chemistry, Biology and Middle years Maths.

I commenced a career in teaching in 2014 and have been working full time as a VCE Biology and Maths teacher in a secondary setting for the last 5 years.

I am currently completing a diploma of IT at Coder Academy, in collaboration with Code Like a Girl. I am IT support in a secondary school, and firmly believe in increasing diversity in tech.
